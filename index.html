Short Bio

Prof. Ziwei Liu is a Nanyang Assistant Professor at School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCSE) in Nanyang Technological University (NTU). Previously, he was a research fellow (2018-2020) in CUHK (with Prof. Dahua Lin) and a post-doctoral researcher (2017-2018) in UC Berkeley (with Prof. Stella X. Yu). His research interests include computer vision, machine learning and computer graphics.

Ziwei received his Ph.D. (2013-2017) from CUHK / Multimedia Lab, advised by Prof. Xiaoou Tang and Prof. Xiaogang Wang. He is fortunate to have internships at Microsoft Research and Google Research.

His previous works include Burst Denoising, CelebA, DeepFashion, DeepMRF, Voxel Flow, Long-Tailed Recognition, and Compound Domain Adaptation.
